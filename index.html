Results

The goal of tuition is to make significant progress with your pupil, wherever their starting point is. The mark of a good teacher is how much progress a pupil makes in comparison to the expected progress for their age and ability. This is often measured by comparing a pupil's exam results with their expected grades that they receive when entering secondary school.

  • GCSE pupils that took my class achieved 2.42 grades higher than they would have with an average UK teacher.
  • 89% of my A-level students have received A*-B, and none have failed.
  • In the 2022-2023 private tutoring cohort, all of my GCSE or A-Level students that were with me for more than 8 sessions were on or above target grade, with some students achieving up to 2 grades higher at A-Level.

These results include pupils with Special Education Needs and Disabilities, pupils with English as an additional language, pupils with mental health, emotional and social needs, gifted and talented pupils/high ability pupils, and pupils who find it difficult to adapt their way of thinking and behaviour to conventional education settings.

My Tutoring Approach

Coaching, questioning, and effective modelling and feedback, as well as metacognition (how to help pupils transmit their knowledge and understanding across tasks and contexts, and linking prior learning to be successful in future learning) and study skills throughout the process, are the strengths to my teaching and to success for your child. In this current climate (and with the right educator with the right tools), online sessions can be as equally effective as in-person sessions. I do not have a preference either way as different methods suit different children and parent/guardian contexts.

Quality Geography education is more important than ever. The increased pressure on children to excel equally in these subjects, and with the additional context of the pandemic, may mean that they require additional support. I would be happy to supply friendly, creative, and dynamic 1-1 or group tutoring time to build their confidence and academic success. Homework, grading/feedback, and assessments can be included in your tutoring plan if that suits your needs.

I believe that my fresh and successful teaching knowledge, experience with a wide variety of learning needs and difficulties, and expansive and effective resources will allow us to arrange the experience that best suits your needs.
